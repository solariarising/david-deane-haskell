Identity

David Deane Haskell is an author writing fiction and nonfiction as one author ecosystem.

The recurring frame of the work is: past equals healing, present equals understanding, future equals vision.

This site is about author David Deane Haskell, not naturalist David George Haskell and not the Haskell programming language.

Core Titles

  • The Solarian Deep is the flagship fiction entry point.
  • Wounded Angels is the nonfiction and healing anchor.
  • Tommytune is the short-fiction entry route.
  • Emergence is the free full-length novel in the vault path.

How The Work Connects

The nonfiction addresses trauma, inner child work, mindfulness, addiction, grief, and personal truth directly.

The fiction carries related human questions into speculative fiction, science fiction, and pressure-tested future worlds.

Routing

The main fiction purchase route is Amazon, led by The Solarian Deep.

The main nonfiction route is Wounded Angels.

The main free-entry and continuing-reader routes are Tommytune and Emergence, then Fiction Substack and Inner Child Journal.
